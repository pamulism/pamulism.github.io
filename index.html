Pamul Yadav

I am a PhD Student at School of Integrated Technology, Yonsei University, working as a graduate researcher at STL Lab under the supervision of Prof. Shiho Kim . My academic activity hovers around the 3 As:

  • AI Safety.
  • Autonomous Vehicles Simulation and its real-world Implementation.
  • Accessibility in HCI.

My main research goal is to develop generalized intelligent vehicles capable of handling unseen situations in real-world. I am actively researching ideas to towards multi-agent approach to attain generalization in Reinforcement Learning.
